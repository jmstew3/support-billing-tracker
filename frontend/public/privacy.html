Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how PeakOne Digital LLC ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information through the Support Billing Tracker application ("the Service"). The Service is an internal business tool used for support request tracking, invoice generation, and QuickBooks Online integration.

2. Information We Collect

The Service processes the following types of information:

  • Support request data: Messages, timestamps, and request details from support conversations.
  • Billing information: Invoice line items, amounts, service hours, and billing rates.
  • Customer records: Company names, contact names, and email addresses associated with client accounts.
  • QuickBooks Online data: Invoice IDs, sync tokens, customer IDs, and item mappings exchanged during the QBO integration.
  • Authentication data: OAuth tokens (encrypted at rest) used to maintain the QuickBooks Online connection.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to operate the Service, including: tracking support requests, calculating billing amounts, generating invoices, and syncing invoice data to QuickBooks Online. We do not use this information for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to the Service's core functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • QuickBooks Online: Invoice, customer, and item data is transmitted to Intuit's QuickBooks Online API as part of the billing sync functionality you authorize.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to a valid legal process.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information, including: AES-256-GCM encryption for OAuth tokens at rest, HTTPS for all data in transit, access-controlled database infrastructure, and authentication requirements for all API endpoints.

6. Data Retention

Support and billing data is retained for as long as needed to provide the Service and to comply with applicable record-keeping requirements. OAuth tokens are automatically refreshed and old tokens are deactivated. You may request disconnection from QuickBooks Online at any time, which will revoke and deactivate stored tokens.

7. Your Rights

As an authorized user of the Service, you may:

  • Request access to data associated with your account.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request disconnection of the QuickBooks Online integration.
  • Contact us with questions about how your data is handled.

8. QuickBooks Online Authorization

When you connect the Service to QuickBooks Online, you authorize the Service to access your QuickBooks Online company data using the com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting scope (read and write access to accounting data). This authorization can be revoked at any time through the Service's disconnect feature or through your Intuit account settings at accounts.intuit.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at support@peakonedigital.com.
