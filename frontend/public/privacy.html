Privacy Policy

PeakOne Digital

Last updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

PeakOne Digital ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information through our Support Billing Tracker platform ("the Service").

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information in the course of providing our services:

  • Support ticket data: Ticket content, timestamps, status, and resolution details synced from Fluent Support
  • Message data: Support-related message content used for request extraction and tracking
  • Billing information: Names, email addresses, invoice amounts, and payment status managed through QuickBooks Online
  • Hosting records: Site names, hosting plan details, and billing cycle information
  • Usage data: Time tracking, request categorization, and service activity logs

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Track and resolve support requests
  • Generate and deliver invoices
  • Calculate billing based on time and service type
  • Provide reporting and analytics on support activity
  • Manage hosting accounts and subscriptions

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a MySQL database hosted on our private infrastructure. We implement the following security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit via TLS
  • Authentication required for all API and dashboard access
  • Database access restricted to the application backend only
  • Regular automated backups
  • Docker container isolation for all service components

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online: Used for invoice generation and delivery. Billing data is transmitted to Intuit's systems. See Intuit's Privacy Statement.
  • Fluent Support: Support ticket data is synced from your Fluent Support instance. Data remains within your WordPress environment and our tracking database.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond what is necessary to provide the Service.

6. Data Retention

We retain support and billing data for as long as needed to provide the Service and meet our business and legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Support tickets and request data are retained for the duration of the service relationship
  • Billing and invoice records are retained for a minimum of seven years for accounting and tax purposes
  • Deleted requests are soft-deleted (marked as inactive) and may be retained in backups

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to our retention obligations
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Service uses session-based authentication. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics services that track individual users across websites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the Service or email. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
